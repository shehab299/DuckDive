Ford Center ID Requirement

The card reader on the main entrance to Ford Center will be active starting on Monday, May 6! Learn more.

Guest Pass Policy Updates

Since Recreation and Wellness transitioned to digital guest passes in 2021, we will no longer accept nor digitize paper guest passes (“red passes”) after Sunday, June 23, 2024. 

Please bring all remaining paper guest passes to any of our front desks for digitization before the deadline. Learn more.

Aquatics

On Saturday, May 11th, the Avery Rec Pool at AOERC will be closed from 12:30 PM - 2:30 PM for a Farm Games event.
We will offer special Youth and Family Access hours on Friday, May 10th, from 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM.

Tennis Court Closures

West Campus

We have recently received feedback that the smell coming from West Campus Courts resurfacing projects (currently underway) is impacting instructors and participants. After consulting with Environmental Health and Safety (EH&S), they strongly recommend moving all activities to different locations until project completion (late May). Therefore, we are sorry to announce that West Campus Courts will be closed for the remainder of the resurfacing project. This includes all events, scheduled practices, and open play for both tennis and pickleball.

Taube South 

Taube South Tennis Courts (577 Campus Drive) is closed until the end of 2024 (unless otherwise specified). Learn more.

Road Closures

Permanent closure of Electioneer Road effective now. Fremont Road will become the main access to Red Barn and the Golf Course.
