Welcome to our improved and expanded 6th grade reading and vocabulary course!

Master reading skills while learning so much more, as you dive into texts that span science, history, and pop culture. Learn about text evidence through the Samurai code, explore words in context through the history of superhero secret identities, and dive into analyzing arguments through the study of the world's first submarine.
