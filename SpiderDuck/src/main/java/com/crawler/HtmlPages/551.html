Frances Loeb Library

The Frances Loeb Library provides the Graduate School of Design, the larger Harvard University community, and the public with resources and services for design research, teaching, and learning.

Search Books & Articles

HOLLIS is Harvard Library's catalog. It searches library resources in a single unified search, including books, articles, media and more. HOLLIS also manages your library loans. You can log in to renew items and check due dates..

Search Other Databases

  • HOLLIS Images

    Harvard Library's image catalog, which includes content from archives, museums, libraries, and other collections throughout Harvard.

  • HOLLIS for Archival Discovery

    Harvard Library's special collections and archives catalog, to help locate primary sources such as letters, photographs, film and video, print items, digital materials, and objects.

  • Material Order SEARCH

    A collaborative online materials catalog that searches the materials collections of the Harvard GSD, Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), and Columbia University GSAPP Preservation Technology Lab (Avery Library).

  • Avery Index to Architectural Periodicals

    A comprehensive listing of journal articles published worldwide on architecture and design, archaeology, city planning, interior design, landscape architecture, and historic preservation.

  • Harvard Geospatial Library

    Harvard Library's repository for regional and local geographic data and georeferenced scanned paper maps.

  • DASH

    A central, open-access repository of research by members of the Harvard community.

African American Design Nexus

The African American Design Nexus (AADN) is a platform that promotes Black designers who have made and are making an indelible contribution to our society and to how we see the world around us. The AADN was developed by the Frances Loeb Library in collaboration with the GSDs African American Student Union.

  • Visit the AADN website
  • Listen to the Nexus Podcast

Building For Tomorrow

In 2017, The Frances Loeb Library received an Institute of Museum and Library Services National Forum Grant to support two meetings of engaged stakeholders architects, architectural historians, archivists, librarians, technologists, and digital preservationists to support long-term preservation of digital design data. Explore the archives and resources created from those meetings.
