Welcome

We look forward to celebrating the inimitable Harvard class of 2024 leading up to and on Thursday, May 23, 2024 for our 373rd Commencement Exercises.

For news coverage and videos of past commencement exercises, please see the links below.

If you have specific questions regarding information that is not covered in the individual graduate or professional school pages (found here), or for college seniors (found here) please contact the Commencement Office at commencement@harvard.edu. Thank you.

Maria Ressa named 2024 Commencement speaker

Maria Ressa, a journalist, media entrepreneur, and leading advocate for freedom of the press, will be the principal speaker at Harvard's 373rd Commencement Exercises on May 23, 2024. 

 

GUESTS OF HARVARD'S CLASS OF 2024

For all guests, a ticket will be required to gain entry into Tercentenary Theatre, the site of Harvard's Commencement Exercises, on Thursday, May 23, 2024.

All persons, bags, and personal items are subject to inspection before entering Harvard Yard. No backpacks of any type will be admitted. No bag or item larger than 12 x 12”x 12” will be permitted inside the Yard. Luggage of any kind is strictly prohibited.

There will be overflow viewing sites around campus and at our students’ Houses and Schools. The proceedings will also be live streamed and available via cable in the local Boston area.
