Harvard Kennedy School Library and Research Services supports the research, learning, and curricular needs of faculty, researchers, students, and staff. It also provides researchers around the world with information related to public policy, public administration, and international affairs.

Lauren Brodsky’s “Because Data Can’t Speak for Itself”

How to galvanize leaders and policymakers to action on the world’s most pressing problems? It’s not with a list of numbers. Instead, it depends on telling compelling stories about real people.

Behind the Book is a video storytelling series that dives into books authored by HKS faculty. Each video distills one book’s critical insights for a worldwide audience.

Political Buttons Collection

The Political Buttons at HKS Collection features 1,600+ buttons that represent U.S. political history from 1904 through today. It includes buttons on candidates, ballot initiatives, social issues, grassroots movements, and more.

Faculty Publications

HKS faculty disseminate their research in publications that contribute to public knowledge and fuel policy innovation. This list features recent faculty publications, including working papers, journal articles, books, edited volumes, research papers, and public testimony.
