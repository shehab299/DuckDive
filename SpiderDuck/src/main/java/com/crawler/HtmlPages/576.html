Gutman Library

For more than 50 years, the Monroe C. Gutman Library has been the premier education library for Harvard University, the greater Boston-Cambridge community, and?educators and education historians everywhere.
Use the Library

Welcome to the Library

As HGSEs social and intellectual heart, Gutman is a hub for innovation, sparking transformative ideas in education. Here, you can search books, explore articles, and ask a librarian.
Learn more about Gutman Library

  • Search HOLLIS

    Search for books and articles using Harvard Library's primary search tool, HOLLIS.

  • Search 3-in-1

    Use 3-in-1 to search education articles. This requires an HUID to login. 

  • FAQ/Ask Us

    Get quick help in multiple ways.

Make an Appointment

Get in-depth one-on-one or small group appointments for students to aid in all their research, writing, technology, and data needs.

  • Research and Digital Scholarship

    Comprehensive research and digital scholarship appointments at all stages of the research process.

  • HGSE Writing Services

    Responding to course assignment prompts, organizing their papers/research projects, or proposals.

  • HGSE Communications Lab (CommLab)

    Assistance with academic or professional presentations, creating an elevator pitch, making research posters, and enhancing a digital brand.

  • HGSE Data Lab

    Information about data in education.
